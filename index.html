Welcome to our site!

Mission Statement

The SEC is here to encourage and promote all aspects of software engineering. We provide social activities and workshops to allow members to learn more about the field as well as engage with fellow collegues.

Who are we?

The Software Engineering Club is a student run organization that promotes a variety of events every year. We have members from Mechatronics Engineering, Software Engineering and Computer Science.
