Software Engineering Club

McMaster University

Mission Statement

The Software Engineering Club is here to encourage members who have a passion for software development. We provide social activities and workshops to allow members to learn more about the field as well as engage with fellow colleagues.

Who are we?

The Software Engineering Club is a student run organization that promotes a variety of events every year. We have members from Mechatronics Engineering, Software Engineering and Computer Science.
